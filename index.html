A Perfect Cup of Tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon loose tea leaves or 1 tea bag
  • 1 teaspoon sugar or honey (optional)
  • Milk (optional)
  • Lemon slice (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil the water in a kettle or saucepan.
  2. If using loose tea leaves, place them in a teapot or infuser. If using a tea bag, place it in your cup.
  3. Pour the boiling water over the tea leaves or tea bag.
  4. Let the tea steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on how strong you like it.
  5. Remove the tea leaves or tea bag.
  6. Add sugar or honey to taste, if desired.
  7. Add a splash of milk or a slice of lemon, if desired.
  8. Stir well and enjoy your perfect cup of tea!
